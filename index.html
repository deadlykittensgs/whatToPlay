What is This For?

This page is intended to make playing music with friends easier not everyone has a personal drummer so this app is made to show chord changes to keep everyone in time and on the same part of the song.

How do I set it up?

just go ahead and type in the first chord you want to play and hit add and it will be added to your q. do this with all the chords in the song and set the tempo and hit start and you are free to go! the song will play on a loop as long as you like. if you want to practice specific parts just click reset and put only those chords in and practice on repeat
